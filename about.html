About us


What is Earthwise Energy?


Earthwise energy is a informative website that aims to communicate the means by which someone can switch to clean energy solutions. It provides basic inforamtion on how various types of renewable energy source work, what incentives there are for people to switch, and how someone can switch. Earthwise Energy was created for the TSA 2023-24 webmaster prompt, our team has researched and compiled information for homeowners on green and clean energy home solutions on this website.
